In the summer of 2025, I joined the production team at [NCCA Jean's Playhouse](https://jeansplayhouse.com) in Lincoln, NH as a scenic carpenter, working under career scenic designer and technical director Andrew Stuart.

The 2025 season - *Jersey Boys* and *Grease* running in repertory - was the highest-grossing musical season in the theatre's history. Working on repertory sets in a professional house gave me hands-on experience with large-scale scenic construction and the fast-paced demands of a working summer stock company.
